# Heritage Respect and Global Scope Note

#CONSHER was created from a natural desire to support:

- the preservation of heritage
- the dignity of surrounding communities
- respectful visitor experience
- cultural harmony
- deeper attention to heritage conservation nationally and internationally

The roadmap emerged after a visit to the Lalibela Churches in Ethiopia.

Some examples may therefore be shaped by Ethiopian heritage events, rituals, local realities, and cultural context.

This local grounding is treated as enrichment.

It is not treated as a limitation of global scope.

#CONSHER is intended as a global heritage-conservation platform roadmap, requiring adaptation, correction, and review by appropriate experts, stakeholders, communities, and institutions in each local context.

The package does not claim authority over Lalibela, Ethiopian heritage, global heritage sites, ritual practice, visitor movement, public safety, conservation policy, or institutional decision-making.

Heritage respect is the motivation.

Domain authority is not claimed.

Public exposure is not authorized.

Public release is not authorized.

GitHub release is not authorized.

Advisory, operational, implementation, deployment, certification, or production use is not authorized.
