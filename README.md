## Heritage respect and global scope

#CONSHER was created from a natural desire to support the preservation of heritage, the dignity of surrounding communities, respectful visitor experience, cultural harmony, and deeper attention to heritage conservation nationally and internationally.

Although the roadmap emerged after a visit to the Lalibela Churches in Ethiopia and may contain examples shaped by Ethiopian heritage events, rituals, and context, #CONSHER is intended as a global heritage-conservation platform roadmap.

Local specificity is treated as grounding and enrichment, not as a limitation of global scope.
