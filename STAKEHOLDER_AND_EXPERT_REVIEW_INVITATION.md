# Stakeholder and Expert Review Invitation

#CONSHER is an early systems roadmap and proof-surface demonstration.

It is explicitly open to correction, expert review, stakeholder feedback, community input, and domain-specific refinement.

The package invites review from:

- heritage conservation experts
- ecclesiastical and ritual-context authorities where applicable
- local community representatives
- heritage authority reviewers
- conservation professionals
- anthropological and historical reviewers
- engineering reviewers where applicable
- public-safety reviewers where applicable
- sustainability reviewers
- governance and institutional-context reviewers

Informal expert interest, appreciation, curiosity, or encouragement must not be interpreted as validation, endorsement, approval, certification, or institutional acceptance.

Any future maturity claim would require documented review, stakeholder dialogue, local institutional context, and domain-specific correction.

This invitation does not authorize public exposure, public release, GitHub publication, LinkedIn announcement, operational use, implementation, deployment, public advisory use, visitor guidance, heritage evaluation, ritual interpretation, or public-safety instruction.
